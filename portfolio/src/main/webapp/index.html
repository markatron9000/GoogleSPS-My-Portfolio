Super Smash Bros Ultimate Optimizer

For my second hackathon, I worked solo, and created an android app that, when told what character your opponent will be playing, tells you the best and worst matchups to counter it! Never pick the wrong character again! It was a little unpolished, but for only having 24 hours, I was very proud of it. Due to the time constraint, I was unable to type all of the matchup lists into the respective character pages. I did get a decent amount of them finished though.
